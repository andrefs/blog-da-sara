Firsts

"Firsts" are always a little weird. Most of the times you have no idea what are you doing or what the outcome will be. Sometimes is just a leap of faith that makes the jump possible. Others it's your heart desire. And at times you don't really see it coming until you're in it.

I've moved to Berlin 5 months ago. It was the first time that I moved out of my country. And I had actually never been to Berlin before so it's hard to explain why I was so excited with the idea some months before deciding. I've always said I wanted to live outside of my country. When I was younger I had this very romantic idea of me travelling across the world with just a tiny little backpack, working in coffee shops, earning money for the next travel, learning new languages and meeting the most interesting people. A regular Kerouac. You know, without the drugs and the sex. And well, the car.

I guess this basic earning never left me, and so here I am. Berlin is so many different things that is kind of hard to put it down in words. I'm a very detail oriented person. For me it's always about the little things. The way people look at you on the subway, how kids behave with each other, the light, the flowers on the table.

I'm not a particularly artsy person, but I've always loved writing. So for many years I had the idea of having a blog. I know it's a bit old fashioned these days. People seem to enjoy fast information more and more. Fewer words, more images. But I love stories, hearing and telling. Nothing beats the joy of sharing something with someone. It makes it more real. The other thing that I love is food. Eating it, cooking it, reading about it, you name it. As long as there’s food involved, I'm in. Well, almost every time.

And that's what I'm hoping to share with whoever reads this (Hi Mum!). Stories. From the city and from the sea. Stories from Berlin, from Portugal, from my favorite places, from my heartaches. And sure, a lot of silliness. Because we also need that.
